Experiment 5: Advanced Ranging Experiment

测距进阶

Source-based English document

This page is an objective English document derived from the corresponding Word manual in the local source folder. The Chinese page keeps the extracted original text, tables, links, commands, and figures.

Purpose

Extend basic range reading into mission rules and obstacle-aware flight actions.

Preparation

  • Crazyflie and Multi-ranger deck.
  • Validated range-reading script.
  • Instructor-confirmed test area.

Procedure

  1. Collect range readings under several obstacle configurations.
  2. Implement obstacle-triggered actions such as landing, backing away, or bouncing.
  3. Adjust thresholds and speeds conservatively.

Verification

  • The program exits through a safe stop condition.
  • Sensor logs support the observed behavior.
  • The selected thresholds can be justified from the measurements.
