Experiment 7: Autonomous Mapping and Review

自主建图+复习

Purpose

Consolidate the ranging and mapping workflow through an autonomous mapping review task.

Preparation

  • Completed range-sensing and mapping exercises.
  • Known test area and flight restrictions.
  • Team notes from previous experiments.

Procedure

  1. Review connection, sensing, control, and mapping requirements.
  2. Design or modify a route that samples useful map areas.
  3. Run the autonomous mapping task and record the result.

Verification

  • The route is reproducible.
  • The produced map or log supports the route explanation.
  • Main risks and improvements are recorded objectively.
