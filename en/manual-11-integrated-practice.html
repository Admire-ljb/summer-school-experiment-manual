Experiment 11: Integrated Practice

阶段综合实践

Purpose

Combine setup, sensing, planning, and control skills in a bounded practical task.

Preparation

  • Completed prior manuals.
  • Task area and constraints.
  • Team role assignment for operation, monitoring, and recording.

Procedure

  1. Read the task constraints and define success criteria.
  2. Break the route or behavior into testable components.
  3. Run the integrated task and record results.

Verification

  • The task is completed within the defined constraints.
  • The team can explain the selected method.
  • Problems are documented with concrete evidence.
